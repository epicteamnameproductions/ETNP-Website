Beats

Beat 1

S.D.C (System's Defense Console)

Lore

Built by P.S.U. to manage most, if not all of their automated systems. It is located at the top of the P.S.U. Headquarters. Despite this, it seems oddly... human. P.S.U. refuses to give more information about, or allow anyone close to it.

Beat 2

Karos Jenkins

Lore

A leading lieutenant for P.S.U., often opting for the extreme solutions to his problems. He always prefers steel over flesh, hence his cybernetic arm. He could've saved that arm but he insisted on replacing it. He then went and replaced most of, if not his whole body despite constant warnings about it.

Beat 3

Paul Modero

Lore

A fitness trainer for P.S.U. He rejected cybernetics for their "lack of skill required" in favor of his natural agility, aside from the respiratory enhancements needed to survive on Earth. He is typically jogging anywhere he can in his free time

Effects

Effect 1

Leah Dansarvi

Lore

A protest leader against the advancement of technology and environmental destruction, despite this fact she is actually rather socially awkward in practice of anything but protesting. Her rebreather was a medical installation for her panic attacks much to her displeasure.

Effect 2

Frank Jamison

Lore

He is Mark's brother, who Mark presumed was dead after a severe car accident. He is the leading manager of F.H.I., and typically spends his time in his lavish home in the outskirts.

Effect 3

Sarena Valentina

Lore

A popular socialite. Typically a figurehead or model for most company branding including the U.E.T.A., P.S.U., or F.H.I. Despite this, she envies Leah's looks to a point of a dynamic rivalry between the two. Almost like they like each other or something.

Effect 4

Lazlo Urkois

Lore

A dropout artist working under the U.E.T.A., he's rather passionate about his creations, typically helping to maintenance the many creations of the association. So much so, that he installed a cybernetic ear that gives him pings when a robot is in any form of need for maintenance
